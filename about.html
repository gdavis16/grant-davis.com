ABOUT

Hi there,

I’m a 23-year-old LEGO fanatic from the Northwestern United States. In my day-to-day life as an Embedded Software Engineer, I daydream constantly about building, thinking up fun build concepts and interesting part connections. When I do find time to build, I am quick to imagine and execute new designs, trying to push the limits of what is thought to be capable with LEGO bricks with the aid of constructive feedback and refinement.

Although I enjoy the more competitive side of building having competed in many contests such as Iron Builder and having traveled to many conventions around the united states, I have most loved the opportunities that I have had to share my LEGO creations with children. There's something special seeing the joy and sense of wonder that LEGO can bring them.

In the rare spare moments that I’m not building, I can be found reading fantasy books, running long distances, playing minecraft, or socializing with friends.

Grant Davis
