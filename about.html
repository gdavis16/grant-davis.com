ABOUT

Hi there,

I’m a 23-year-old LEGO fanatic from the Northwestern United States. In my day-to-day life as an Embedded Software Engineer, I daydream constantly about building, thinking up fun build concepts and . When I do find time to build, I am quick to imagine and execute new designs, constantly trying to push the limits of what is thought to be capable with LEGO bricks.

I have been involved in the LEGO fan community for 9 years now, having competed in many competitions online, such as Iron Builder, and building at many LEGO conventions around the US. I love being able to work with other builders on projects and hear their thoughts about what makes a good creation.

In the rare spare moments that I’m not building, I can be found reading fantasy books, running, playing minecraft, or socializing with friends.

Grant Davis
